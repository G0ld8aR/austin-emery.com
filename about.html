About Me

My background and how I got into cybersecurity.

I am a Navy veteran with an AS in Cybersecurity from City College of San Francisco and I am currently working toward my BS in Cybersecurity at DeVry University. My path into cloud security did not start with theory only. It has been built through military experience, technical troubleshooting, internships, labs, and project work that forced me to learn by doing.

What I enjoy most is the mix of analysis and hands on implementation. I like building out a lab, documenting the process, troubleshooting what breaks, and then tightening the result until it feels useful instead of just finished. That is a big part of what this portfolio is meant to show.

A lot of my work leans toward cloud security, infrastructure, monitoring, network fundamentals, and technical write ups. I tend to keep pushing a project further than the original assignment because I want it to become something worth showing later. That mindset is what shaped this site too.

What visitors should take away

I am building toward roles where practical security thinking matters. I like clean documentation, steady troubleshooting, and work that balances technical depth with real operational value.
